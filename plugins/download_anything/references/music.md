# Music & Audio Resources

## Free & Open Music
- Free Music Archive (freemusicarchive.org — CC-licensed)
- Jamendo (jamendo.com — independent artists, CC-licensed)
- ccMixter (ccmixter.org — remixes, CC-licensed)
- Musopen (musopen.org — public domain classical)
- Internet Archive Audio (archive.org — public domain recordings)

## Independent Artist Platforms
- Bandcamp (bandcamp.com — user's own purchased content)
- SoundCloud (soundcloud.com — CC-licensed and artist-authorized downloads)

## Podcasts & Spoken Word
- Public podcast RSS feeds
- LibriVox (librivox.org — public domain audiobooks)
- Internet Archive

## Tools
- yt-dlp -x --audio-format mp3 URL (extract audio from public videos)
- spotdl (user's own Spotify playlists)
